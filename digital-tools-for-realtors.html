MileIQ

MileIQ has definitely made tracking mileage easier. The best way to use this app is to allow Location to be enabled. This way the app can track your drives. I update my drives at least once a week. If you forget to open your MileIQ app after a while, the app has a great feature that shows your locations on two maps shown side by side. I have to cycle through each trip and label them Personal or Business. With the MileIQ app you can create your own labels. For my business drives I have a couple labels created: Showing Property, Closing, Supplies, etc. The app is not free. I usually refrain from spending money on apps, but this one has been worth it to me. Also, come tax time, MileIQ creates spreadsheets automatically that you can send to your accountant (or yourself if you do your own taxes). This is one of my favorite things about MileIQ
